About TrackDX

TrackDX exists for one reason: to help patients gain control of their condition. This tool was designed by people who also have chronic health conditions. Too many times we would go to the doctor and not be able to tell them definitively if a medication was working. Further, we had our own questions of what was triggering an episode. TrackDX takes the guesswork out of this and provides you and your doctor the answers you need.

Our philosophy

  • We believe your data is yours. We don't collect it, share it, or sell it. It stays on your device — though you can export it to your computer if you want.
  • We believe we are not your doctor. Unlike apps that attempt to diagnose you, we don't think that's ethical, and we reserve that judgment for your medical professional.
  • We believe you deserve access to insights without paying a high price. Having a condition is expensive enough as is. That's why we charge a minimal, one-time fee. Because subscriptions suck.

About TrackDX

TrackDX exists for one reason: to put patients back in control of their own health data. Managing a condition day to day is hard enough without also having to remember, weeks later, what triggered which episode or whether a medication is actually helping. Most tools ask you to fit your condition into someone else's template. TrackDX doesn't — it's built to track whatever you're living with, on your terms, and to turn the record you keep into evidence you and your doctor can actually act on.

We believe patients do better when they're empowered with their own data — not overwhelmed by it, and not left to guess. TrackDX is designed to make logging fast enough to stick with, and the insights clear enough to be useful at your next appointment, not just interesting to look at.
